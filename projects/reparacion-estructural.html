Phase 01 / Demolition & Preparation

Slab Remediation & Compromised Material Removal

Addressing structural failures in the original concrete slab caused by impact or extreme loading, we executed precise diamond blade perimeter sawing followed by deep mechanical demolition. Defective materials were thoroughly cleared, and the sub-grade was re-leveled to eliminate deflection issues prior to the new pour.

Phase 02 / Reinforcement & Pouring

Structural Steel Grid Integration

To ensure the newly reconstructed patch withstands high-impact industrial loads and continuous heavy forklift operations, an engineered steel reinforcement framework was integrated. High-density concrete was then technically poured and micro-leveled using professional magnesium hand floats to achieve a flush, flat transition with the existing surface.
